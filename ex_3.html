Indian remote sensing

The third satellite in the series Indian Remote Sensing(IRS) satellite series was IRS - 1C which was launched in December, 1995 has three sensors:

  1. A single-channel panchromatic (PAN)
  2. A medium resolution four-channel Linear Imaging Self-scanning Sensor(LISS-III)
  3. A coarse resolution two-channel Wide Field Sensor(WiFS)
